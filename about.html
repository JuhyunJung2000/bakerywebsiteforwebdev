About us.

Our Story

This amazing bakery started in the home of Mr Bakers...

During the 90's Mr baker had lost his job as a salesman, he had nothing to do one day and decided to make bread, the bread was very good. The news spread about the bread and he started to get many requests for them. Then he opened a shop in Central London first on October 24th 1998. Today there are four main shops in Europe.. it has become the favorite bakery of many. You might have seen the menu. it may not be the most enticing. What's with just a croissant? well people who have tried say " I can not go back to having other croissants.". There secret recipe of the Baker's will be revealed one day but for now it will not be disclosed.
